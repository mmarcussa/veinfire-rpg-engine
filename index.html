How to Play
  1. Start a new chat with the selected AI model.
  2. Let the AI try the GitHub-hosted Markdown canon links first. If it cannot access them, use Download All Canon Files or the individual download buttons, then upload the files manually to the AI chat.
  3. Click Build GM Packet.
  4. Click Copy Packet.
  5. Paste the packet into the new AI chat.
  6. Tell the AI: Start the session.
  7. Play by typing what your character does.
  8. When the GM gives tracker changes, paste them into the Campaign Tracker tab or edit the trackers manually.
