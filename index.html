Welcome to the VEINFIRE RPG Engine

This engine helps you prepare a heavy VEINFIRE / Eldoria roleplaying session for an AI GM. You will build a canon-aware character, generate a fresh campaign premise, and create a structured GM handoff packet that tells your chosen AI how to run the opening session.

The engine is not the live GM. It is the setup table: character sheet, campaign seed, canon handoff, and optional tracker tools. Once the packet is copied, the story continues in your AI chat as a narrated RPG session.

Track progress separatelyUse Campaign Tracker from Tools when you want to import GM updates, inventory, NPCs, and consequences.
